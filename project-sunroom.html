The Sunroom Retreat

Location

Virginia

Scope

Living & sunroom redesign

Services

Interior design · Furnishings · Window treatments

A wall of windows, a beadboard ceiling, and furniture soft enough to sink into — a room built for slow mornings.

The brief was simple and hard at once: make a bright, window-lined room feel warm rather than exposed. Layered window treatments do most of the work — woven shades for texture and softness, floral drapery panels to frame the glass without closing it off.

Furnishings stay quiet and tactile: a generous slipcovered sofa, curved swivel chairs, a woven rug, and a brass-framed bench that keeps the centre of the room open. The palette borrows from what is already outside the windows.
