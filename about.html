About Julie

Founder & principal designer

Helping families create homes they never want to leave.

For me, design has always been personal. I love getting to know a family — how they gather, how they unwind, what makes a house feel like theirs — and shaping spaces around that.

With more than 30 years in the industry, I bring a practiced eye, deep trade relationships, and a highly personal approach to every project — rooted in natural materials, thoughtful craftsmanship, and the belief that a home should feel both beautiful and truly livable.

Julie Davis-GerardFounder & Principal Designer · ASID Allied Member
The story

Since launching her custom furniture and interior design career in 1990, Julie has built an unusually broad base of experience — from bespoke furniture and full interior design to custom-built homes, renovations, and every step in between.

That range is the point. Because she came up through furniture and millwork as well as interiors, Julie is as fluent in cabinetry, layout, and construction detail as she is in fabrics, lighting, and art. Clients get one designer who can sit comfortably in a job-site conversation about framing and finish schedules, then turn around and select the textiles that make a room feel like home.

Beyond The Mill Design believes in honing the craft and collaborating with the best architects, builders, tradespeople, and suppliers — partners who care about quality and communication in equal measure. Those long-standing relationships are why projects run smoothly, and why so much of the studio's work comes from returning clients and referrals.

Julie is an Allied Member of ASID, the American Society of Interior Designers, and her work has been featured in multiple publications. Above all, she's known for listening carefully, keeping her clients' needs at the forefront, and making the design process feel thoughtful, collaborative, and genuinely enjoyable.

How we work

Thoughtful, organized, and genuinely fun.

A renovation shouldn't feel intimidating. Our process is built to keep you informed and involved — without putting the weight of a thousand decisions on your shoulders.

01

Consultation

It starts with a conversation in your home — how you live, what you love, what you want it to feel like. No pressure, lots of listening.

02

Design & sourcing

Layouts, finishes, custom millwork, and furnishings come together into one clear vision — sourced and managed so you don't have to.

03

Build & reveal

We work shoulder to shoulder with our trusted trades to bring it to life, then hand you back a home that feels finished, personal, and entirely yours.
