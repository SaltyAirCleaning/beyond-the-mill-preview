Transitional Kitchen & Living

Location

Virginia

Scope

Kitchen & open-plan renovation

Services

Renovation design · Cabinetry · Lighting · Furnishings

A classic white kitchen opened to the rooms around it — built to cook in, gather in, and live in.

The renovation reworked the plan so the kitchen, dining, and sitting areas read as one space. A custom hood and full-height cabinetry give the range wall presence, while a generous island in a contrasting wood warms the palette and seats the whole family.

Black shaded pendants and a mix of seating keep the room from feeling like a showroom. Beyond the island, a soft sitting area makes the kitchen somewhere people linger rather than pass through.
