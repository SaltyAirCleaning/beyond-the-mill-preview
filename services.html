What we do

Services

Design that understands how a home is built.

Beyond The Mill is as comfortable with the construction conversation as with fabrics and finishes. We guide the whole home — layout, cabinetry, materials, lighting, and scale through to furnishings and the final styled details — in close collaboration with trusted builders and trades.

01 — Full service

Full-Service Interior Design

Cohesive design for the whole home, from first concept through the final styled room. We handle the vision and the thousand decisions underneath it, so the result feels collected rather than assembled.

  • Space planning and floor plans
  • Finish, material, and colour direction
  • Furniture plans, sourcing, and procurement
  • Installation and final styling
02 — Renovation

Renovation & Remodel Design

The decisions that happen before the pretty part: layout, flow, structure, and finishes. We speak the builder's language and stay involved through construction, so design intent survives contact with the job site.

  • Layout and floor-plan reconfiguration
  • Finish schedules and material specification
  • Lighting plans and electrical placement
  • Collaboration with builders, architects, and trades
03 — Kitchens & baths

Kitchens & Baths

The rooms that ask the most of a design. Cabinetry, stone, tile, hardware, and lighting chosen to work as hard as they look — and detailed properly so they last.

  • Cabinetry design and custom millwork
  • Stone, tile, and plumbing selection
  • Appliance planning and ergonomics
  • Vanities, built-ins, and storage
04 — Furnishings

Furnishings & Styling

Furniture, textiles, lighting, and art — the layer that turns a finished house into a home you want to sink into. Comfortable, durable, and chosen for the way your family actually lives.

  • Furniture selection and custom upholstery
  • Window treatments and textiles
  • Rugs, lighting, and art curation
  • Final styling and accessorizing
05 — Custom

Custom Millwork & Furniture

Where the name comes from. Built-ins, paneling, and bespoke pieces designed for your space and made by craftspeople we've worked with for years.

  • Built-in cabinetry, shelving, and banquettes
  • Paneling, trim, and architectural detail
  • Custom furniture design
  • Finish and hardware detailing
