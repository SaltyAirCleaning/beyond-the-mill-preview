Landsdowne Home Design

Location

Landsdowne, Virginia

Scope

Full-home design

Services

Interior design · Furnishings · Window treatments · Lighting

A two-storey great room anchored by hand-laid stone — furnished so a room that tall still feels like somewhere you can put your feet up.

The challenge in a room with this much volume is scale. A generous sectional, oversized tufted ottomans, and a substantial iron-and-wood table hold their own beneath the height, while a wrought-iron chandelier bridges the gap between the seating and the arched windows above.

Layered neutrals and a traditional patterned rug keep the palette warm against all that stone. Beyond the great room, the kitchen and breakfast area were furnished to match — wood cabinetry, stone counters, and pendant lighting that carries the same hand.
