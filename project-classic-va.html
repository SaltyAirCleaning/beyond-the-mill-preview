Virginia

Classic Virginia Home

Location

Virginia

Scope

Full-home design

Services

Interior design · Lighting · Custom millwork · Furnishings

A traditional Virginia home given room to breathe — stone, timber, and tailored upholstery, warmed by custom lighting.

The main living and dining rooms were treated as one connected space. A stone fireplace anchors the seating area, while a coffered ceiling and a long linear fixture draw the eye across to the dining table.

Classic silhouettes — tufted wing chairs, cane-back dining seating, a leather club chair — sit against quiet walls so the architecture and the millwork stay the loudest thing in the room. In the entry, a sculptural glass fixture turns the staircase into a moment.
