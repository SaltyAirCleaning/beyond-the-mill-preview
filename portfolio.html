A curated look at recent work — full-home renovations, kitchens, custom millwork, and the finishing details that make a house feel lived-in. Each project is shaped around the family who lives there.

Southwest Florida Coastal Home

Southwest Florida · Full-home design & renovation

Vaulted ceilings, white oak, and a wall of glass to the water. The kitchen, dining, and living areas open into one generous space anchored by a marble waterfall island — then every room beyond it, from the arched-mirror primary bath to the fitted closet, was given the same care.
